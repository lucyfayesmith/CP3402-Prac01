About Us

This product is designed as a little thimble-like object that you slip onto a new borns finger while they are sleeping. This "thimble" tracks the baby's pulse and sends the data to an app which is loaded onto the parent or caretakers phone.

In the event of the baby's pulse decreasing to unsafe levels or possibly stopping, an alarm is sounded from the parents/caretakers phone. This product will be designed to reduce accidental suffocation of babies while sleeping, prevent Sudden Infant Death syndrome (SIDS), etc by providing the parents with the piece of mind that comes with the ease of checking their baby's heart rate
